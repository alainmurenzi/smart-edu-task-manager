General Questions

SMART Edu Task Manager is a comprehensive web-based platform designed to streamline task management in educational institutions. It connects administrators, teachers, and students, allowing for efficient task creation, assignment, submission, and tracking.

The platform is designed for three types of users:
  • Administrators: Manage users, classes, subjects, and view analytics
  • Teachers: Create tasks, assign them to students, and review submissions
  • Students: View assigned tasks, submit completed work, and track progress

Yes, SMART Edu Task Manager is completely free to use for educational institutions. There are no hidden fees or premium features.

Account & Registration

Click on "Register" in the navigation menu and select "As Teacher". Fill in your details and submit the form. Note that an administrator will need to assign classes to you before you can start creating tasks.

Click on "Register" in the navigation menu and select "As Student". Fill in your details, including your class assignment. Once approved by an administrator, you'll be able to access your tasks.

Yes, if you forget your password, contact your administrator who can help you reset it. For security reasons, password reset functionality is currently managed by administrators.

Using the Platform

Teachers can create tasks by navigating to their dashboard and clicking "Create New Task". Fill in the task details including title, description, deadline, priority, and select the class and subject. You can also attach files to the task.

Students can view their assigned tasks on their dashboard. Click on "Submit" for the relevant task, write your submission text or upload files, and click "Submit Assignment".

Teachers can click "Review" on any task they've created to see all student submissions. They can then grade or provide feedback on each submission.

Tasks can be assigned one of four priority levels:
  • Urgent & Important: Must be completed immediately
  • High Priority: Should be completed soon
  • Standard tasks>Medium Priority:
  • Low Priority: Optional or long-term tasks

Technical Support

SMART Edu Task Manager works best on modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated to the latest version.

The platform supports common file formats including PDF, DOCX, images (JPG, PNG), and text files. The maximum file size is 16MB per upload.
