How the Platform Works

1. Register & Login

Teachers and students register with their roles and log in to access the platform.

2. Create & Assign Tasks

Teachers create tasks with priorities, deadlines, and assign them to students or classes.

3. Complete & Submit

Students view prioritized tasks, complete them, and submit for review. Teachers monitor progress.
